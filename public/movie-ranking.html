Top movies according to Dayne

Dayne's Top Movies

1. The Batman (2022)

The reason why this is the greatest movie ever is because this is the best depicted Batman there is.
He is a lonely, emo night owl (bat) that seems to hate other people. The Ridler might be one of the best
one screen villians we have seen. It also sets up and includes a lot of other villians as well.

2. The Dark Knight (2008)

Need I explain more? Now this was my favorite movie up until the Batman came out. Heath Ledger as the
Joker is undefeated and no one will ever top his performance, but the cinematics and plot does not match
to The Batman which is why it is second.

3. Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith (2005)

Now this is arguably the best Star Wars movie, although many will say the original trilogy is much better,
however, the fight scenes and plot that goes down in this movie tops anything that we had seen before.
No matter what your opinions are on the other two movies in the prequals, it should not carry into this movie.
This is the greatest Star Wars movie ever.
